Decisions With Confidence

Information is shared organization-wide the moment it is loaded. Through remote connections, field personnel have access to all information, further strengthening the decision process. Information flows seamlessly from one part of the organization to the next, eliminating duplicate entry. Managers, engineers, geologists, foremen and support staff share the same data to complete the task at hand.

Many activities that used to take considerable time can be performed in seconds. A task once requiring the collection of data from many sources can be performed effortlessly.

In Oil & Gas, decisions can have million-dollar implications. inerG’s data cloud adhering to industry standards gives you a platform for deriving timely insights. Our customers can now make smart decisions efficiently and on time with confidence.

Pays For Itself

Oil & Gas operators are constantly faced with problems and not enough time to address them. Even though the playing field is always shifting, the fundamental goal has remained the same - optimize operations by reducing costs and improving recovery. By integrating economic, production, operational and market data into common data platform, inerG helps unearth inefficiencies and identify operational changes and risk mitigation strategies that can potentially save hundreds of thousands of dollars each year and improve margins. Many clients immediately see a return on their investment.
